Purpose

The purpose for Pete's Icecream Shop to have a website is to reach those that enjoy icecream and the many flavors that there are. We hope that by having this website that it will bring in more customers, make easier, and more convient for customer. Not only that we hope that the site is enticing and will help customers know what Pete's Icecream Shop has to offer.

Audience

The audience that we would like to reach are from children to retired customers. That enjoy sweets such as icecream and are looking for something new to try. Pete's Icecream Shop website will be able to be found on any search engine and any electronic device.

About Us

Pete’s Ice cream shop was started the 21st of June 1960 by Peter Malone in Houston, Texas. For years the Malone family made homemade ice cream and on many of occasions would bring it to community activities through the years. Peter’s family ice cream has through community activities became famous throughout the county. The Malone family owned a farm and for many years their farm did not do well. When Peter graduated from school, and he knew that his family needed his help to provide for the family so they can keep their farm. Knowing that his family’s homemade ice cream was well liked throughout the county brought the idea to start a family ice cream shop. Although he knew that he could not do it on his own. As Peter looked into financing the ice cream shop, he recruited a local businessman by the name of John Lockwood. They both decided that it would be best to start locally as their funds were low. As they started up, they served the common flavors of ice cream during the 60s such as vanilla and chocolate. After a couple of years of business Peter Malone and John Lockwood expanded to near by towns and started to advertise through the news papers. After many years managing the shop Peter past it down to his son and retired.

Price and Container Options

Like other shops we carry containers sizes in small, medium and large. We also sell it in cartons in sizes from 8oz to 16oz. We also carry sugar cones, waffle cones and chocolate dip waffle cones. A small costs an extra of 0.50 cents. Medium costs 1.00 dollar. A large cost 2.00 dollars. An 8oz carton costs around 5 dollars per carton. The 16oz costs about 20 dollars for each carton. One scoop of ice cream costs 1.67 dollar per scoop of ice cream. Up to three scoops of ice cream will cost 5.01 dollars not including tax. Pete’s Ice Cream shop offer sales seasonally for the common holidays that are celebrated. We also offer sales on ice cream that is more than a week or two old and when there is an over abundance of ice cream that was made for the week. Not only that we also offer daily coupons depending on the type of ice cream. Costs may vary depending on the cost of labor and the amount each ingredient. Pete’s Ice cream shop will have a monthly raffle for 50% off any container or carton. Not only that if they get the store reward card they can get in store sales.
