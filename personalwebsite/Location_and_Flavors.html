Location & Flavors

Pete's Ice cream shop can be found on the corner of North Hanworth’s and Johnson Street. From two flavors to twenty. All our ice creams are made in the shop, making sure that none of our products are not old. The ice cream flavors include:

  • Vanilla(made with fresh cream)
  • Chocolate(made with real chocolate)
  • Mint Chocolate Chip(made with fresh mint)
  • Strawberry(with fresh strawberries)
  • Honeycomb
  • Cookies and Cream
  • Cookie Dough
  • Bubble Gum
  • Strawberry Cheesecake
  • Huckleberry(made with fresh huckleberries)
  • Peanut Buttercup
  • Moose Tracks
  • Coffee
  • Death by Chocolate
  • Peppermint
  • Raspberry(made with fresh raspberries)
  • Rocky Road
  • Sherbet
  • Cherry Chocolate chip
  • Birthday Cake

Pete’s Ice Cream shop is always looking for new flavors to try. Please let us know what you would like to see in our shop. With your help we can expand to knew horizons with your ideas of how we may expand. As we get suggestions, we will give each a trial basis before making it permanent. This will be decide through how well it sells in our ice cream shop.
