Location & Flavors

Pete's Ice cream shop can be found on the corner of North Hanworth’s and Johnson Street. From two flavors to twenty. All our ice creams are made in the shop, making sure that none of our products are not old. The ice cream flavors include:

    Chocolate(made with real chocolate)
    Mint Chocolate Chip(made with fresh mint)

    Honeycomb

    Strawberry Cheesecake

    Peanut Butter cup
    Moose Tracks
    Coffee

    Raspberry(made with fresh raspberries)
    Rocky Road
    Sherbet
